Computing Plotline

Timeline of Computing Technologies in the Film

Opening Scene (~0:00–0:05)
Digital News and Government Propaganda Screens: Large screens in public spaces broadcast the death of “Baby Diego” and government messaging.

Theo Walks Through the City (~0:06–0:10)
Holographic and Digital Advertising: 3D ads and digital signage dominate buses and walls.
Surveillance and Security Technology: CCTV, drones, and facial recognition monitor civilians.

Theo at Work (~0:11–0:15)
Digital News and Messaging: State-controlled news and updates flow continuously through digital terminals.

Jasper's Home (~0:25–0:35)
Communication Tech: Jasper accesses pirate broadcasts and avoids state surveillance using analog means.

Kee’s Pregnancy Revealed (~0:45)
Fertility Testing and Medical Scanners: Portable scanner reveals Kee’s pregnancy via advanced imaging.

Journey to Refugee Camp (~1:00–1:20)
Refugee Containment and Tracking: Barcoded IDs, cages, biometric systems control refugee movement.
Military and Police Drones: Automated aerial surveillance tracks rebel and refugee activities.

Inside Bexhill Camp (~1:20–1:40)
Armored and Futuristic Vehicles: Retrofitted buses and police convoys manage refugee transport and combat situations.

Climax – Baby Revealed (~1:45)
Surveillance Tech and Ceasefire: Soldiers stop fighting at the sight of Kee’s baby, momentarily overriding technological aggression with human awe.

Final Scene (~1:50)
Hope vs Tech: Kee and the baby escape via boat—a low-tech method symbolic of renewal in contrast to the oppressive digital landscape.
